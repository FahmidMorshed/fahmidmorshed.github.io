Research

My research interest is to understand Pedagogical Planning in AI-Driven Learning Environemnts using Reinforcement Learning, Deep Learning, Multimodal Learning, Data Mining, and Machine Learning techniques. My work focuses on learning personalized scaffolds in terms of hints, feedback, and explanations leveraging limited-sized dataset with deep Reinforcement learning techiniqes while understanding student behaviors through multimodal channels and machine learning. My disseration is on online RL framework for AI-driven learning environments where I focus on creating a unified framework for devising personalized pedagogical support through branching narrative in game-based learning environments. I believe AI will play a pivotal role on shaping next-generation education for supporting students by contextualizing individial needs to maximize their potential in terms of engagement and learning.

I am currently working with off-policy Deep Reinforcement Learning on Tutorial Planning Agent using real world Amazon Mechanical Turk data. This project studies ICAP model of cognitive psychology to understand learning gain. The goal is to produce effective remediation policies using only historical data (without an environment to act).
